TABLETOP AFICIONADOS


A site to search and rate various tabletop boardgames from our database

About

Tabletop Aficionados is a site for people to search and review their favorite boardgames. We already have a substantial database of boardgames within our site that is being updated constantly by the site's community. If there isn't a game a user can fin, however, anyone can create a listing of a boardgame and review for it. This site is a gathering of the casual or hardcore players of tabletop games. So feel free to explore the various boardgames on our site in order to plan for the next family gathering or night in!

Policy

No form of inappropriate reviews as well as creation of fake or inaccurate tabletop games will be allowed for posting. Each review and tabletop listing will be reviewed by our admins before being allowed to be posted. In terms of being qualified to being a tabletop game on the site, it must have a retail price and a game actually sold to or released to the public in some shape or form.
